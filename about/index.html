Our Mission- to nurture your zest for life so you can be the team you want to be.

We at Unplugged know that the monotony of everyday life can get you feeling like you're stuck in a never ending cycle of work, sleep, and repeat. The goal of our team is to break that cycle and get your team back in a positive headspace.

Through the healing power of nature, we will help your team decompress and reconnect with themselves, each other, and the world around you. We will help you build a collection of tools that your company can use to provide a happy and positive work environment.

Contact us, everyone deserves a break.
