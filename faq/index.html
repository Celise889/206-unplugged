Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

There are plenty of billboards along the highway to point you in the right direction, but we will include a map in your itinerary for added piece of mind.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Any company or team that is wanting to take a time out from the every day stresses of work, and nurture a stronger bond through relaxation and exciting team building activities, would be a perfect fit for Unplugged.

What size teams can you accommodate?

We have private rooms at our location to comfortably accommodate up to 12 people.

Does "Unplugged" really mean that there is no wifi available?

We have a dedicated computer room with wifi available for your team members to use, but we ask that guests try to refrain from spending too much time using the computers as it hinders relaxation.
